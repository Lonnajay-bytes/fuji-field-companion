# Credits

## Recipe preview source photographs

The recipe previews are derivative color grades made from reusable sample photographs distributed with open-source software packages installed in the build environment.

- `skimage.data.coffee`, `skimage.data.chelsea`, `skimage.data.rocket`, and `skimage.data.stereo_motorcycle` from scikit-image sample data.
- Example photographs from TeX Live packages including `uantwerpendocs`, `sduthesis`, and `stellenbosch-2`.

The source photographs are included under `assets/sources/` and each recipe can toggle between its graded preview and source image.

## Live data and maps

- Weather, forecast, sunrise, sunset, and geocoding: Open‑Meteo.
- Map display: Leaflet.
- Map tiles and geographic data: OpenStreetMap contributors.
